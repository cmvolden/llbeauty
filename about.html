Rosario Lavy

With over 30 years experienced as a Professional Make-Up Artist, becoming a Permanent Make-up Artist was a natural career progression.

Rosario is trained Nationally and Internationally, and continues her education to always be able to offer the latest techniques and products to her clients. She has many certifications in Eyelash Extensions and was among the very first 20 in North America to be trained in Russian Volume.

She was featured in The Ottawa Citizen and Ottawa Life Magazine.

Her commitment to her craft shows in the details, Rosario is always trying to enhance the natural beauty of her clients while maintaining a fresh look.

Her ultimate goal is to become a Paramedical Tattoo Artist.
